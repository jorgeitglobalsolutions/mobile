Fitness App MVP (Phase 1) - Quotation & Milestones
Project Overview
Development of a streamlined fitness mobile application focused on daily usability, training tracking, and a built-in subscription model. The scope is optimized for fast launch and real-world validation.
Scope of Work
•	Mobile Application (iOS & Android):
- Workout tracking (exercises, sets, reps, weight)
- Predefined routines
- Custom routine creation (simple)
- Workout history (date, duration, exercises, weight, reps, best set)
- Basic habit tracking (protein, water)
- Push notifications (simple reminders)
•	Subscription System:
- 7-day free trial
- Monthly subscription
- User states (trial, active, expired)
- Paywall after expiration
- App Store & Google Play integration
•	Backend:
- Firebase (Authentication, Firestore)
- Subscription validation logic
- Data storage and management
